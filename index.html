Undoubtedly, he's killed people who he considered to be unworthy of his time and attention. Yet, Hisoka never harms others for fun.
Hisoka also demonstrates decent skill with Enhancement, Emission, and Manipulation Nen. He is an incredibly balanced fighter.
Hisoka simply named his abilities, Bungee Gum and Texture Surprise, after his favorite brands of gum and candy, respectively.
